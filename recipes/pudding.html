Custurd Pudding

A healthy dose of happiness, deliciousness, wholesomeness and sweetness. Custurd pudding is widely regarded as the king of the pudding world, where it is a just and a kind ruler for its subjects.

Custurd pudding, as the name suggests, has its ingredients revolving around the main ingredient which is the Custurd or custurd poweder. It brings a soft, jelly-like and a smooth texture to the already soft, jelly-like and a smooth dish, making it extra soft, jelly-like and overall smooth and soothing.

    The main ingredients are as follows:
  • Custurd powder
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla essence
  • Fruits such as a banana(optional)
  • Caramel
  • Bread
    The steps to be followed are as follows:
  1. Take the custurd powder, milk, water, sugar and bread crumbs and mix them thoroughly.
  2. Boil the mixture on low flame and let it solidify a little
  3. Add a spoonful of vanilla essence to the mixture
  4. Turn the flame to low after a few minutes
  5. Let the mixture cool down to the room temperature, and then keep in the fridge for 6 hours
  6. Optional step: Coat the container of the mixture with caramel beforehand
